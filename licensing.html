Licensing Options

Standard License

Ideal for web use, including websites, social media, and online videos. Perfect for independent creators, small businesses, and digital projects.

  • Worldwide, perpetual use
  • Websites and social media posts
  • Online video (YouTube, Vimeo, etc.)
  • No use in broadcast television or film
  • No use in physical products for resale

Extended License

For large-scale commercial projects, including broadcast television, feature films, and advertising campaigns. The ultimate license for professional productions.

  • Includes all Standard License rights
  • Broadcast television and VOD platforms
  • Feature films and theatrical releases
  • Use in merchandise and physical products
