Animation & Motion Outline

The animation is envisioned as a seamless, majestic journey. The camera will execute a slow, graceful drift, as if floating alongside the celestial creatures. The primary motion will be the serene, slow-motion swimming of the giant whales, their bodies creating gentle ripples in the cosmic ether. Transitions between scenes will be soft and fluid, often using the passing of a whale or a dense star cloud as a natural wipe. Ethereal light rays will pierce through nebula clouds, and the bioluminescence of the whales will pulse softly, illuminating their stellar surroundings.
