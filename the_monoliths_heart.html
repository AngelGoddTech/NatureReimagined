The Monolith's Heart

A massive, black cube hovers in a vibrant rainforest, transforming organic life into glowing geometric wireframes before absorbing them into its void-like surface.

Final Cinematic

Animation & Motion Outline

The animation is defined by a slow, ominous, and hypnotic rhythm. A very slow camera push towards the monolith establishes its imposing presence. The core effect is the transformation: as a plant or creature nears the cube, it will be scanned by a thin line of light. Following the scan, its organic form will seamlessly dissolve into a glowing, cyan wireframe of itself. This wireframe will hold its shape for a moment before being pulled apart into streams of light and data, which are then absorbed into the monolith's perfectly black surface. The motion is deliberate and unsettling, contrasting the vibrant life of the jungle with the cold, digital process of the monolith.
